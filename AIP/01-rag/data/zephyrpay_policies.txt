ZephyrPay Refund and Support Policy (Internal Document v3.2)

ZephyrPay is a fictional digital wallet used only for this RAG demo. Nothing here is real.

1. Refund window
   Customers can request a refund within 14 calendar days of a transaction.
   Refunds for "ZephyrGold" premium subscriptions are allowed only within 7 days.
   After the window closes, refunds require manager approval (code MGR-APPROVE).

2. Refund processing time
   Standard refunds are processed within 3 to 5 business days.
   Refunds to international cards take 7 to 10 business days due to network settlement.

3. Failed transactions
   If money is debited but the transaction failed, it is auto-reversed within 48 hours.
   If not reversed in 48 hours, the customer must raise ticket type "AUTO-REVERSAL-MISS".

4. Support hours
   Live chat support runs 6 AM to 11 PM IST, seven days a week.
   Phone support runs 9 AM to 6 PM IST on business days only.
   Email support has a 24-hour first-response SLA.

5. Account limits
   A new unverified account has a wallet limit of 10,000 rupees.
   After KYC verification, the limit increases to 200,000 rupees.
   Daily transfer limit for verified accounts is 100,000 rupees.
